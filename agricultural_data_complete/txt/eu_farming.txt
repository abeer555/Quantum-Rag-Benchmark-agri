Vision for Agriculture and Food

 European Union 2025 - Adobe Stock - Taras Rudenko
Ensuring the future of our farmers and food
Recognising the essential role of farmers in our lives, the Vision seeks to boost the agri-food sectors competitiveness and attractiveness.
It outlines the enabling conditions to ensure the sector thrives, innovates, and continues benefiting society today, tomorrow and in 2040.

Young farmers
!three young farmers walking in a cow shed
Only 11 of farm holdings in the EU are run by people under the age of 40, and persuading young people to get into farming is a challenge.
Faced with an ageing farming population, the EU is stepping up its efforts to encourage young people into farming.
